Notation. You can use the standard mathematical notation, + - * /, and ^ for exponentation. Functions are written as sin(x) (cos, tan, log, sqrt). Greek letters are written in full: theta, Omega (for a capital omega). Use brackets to change the precedence of operators: x + 5 * 6 is different from (x + 5) * 6.

Special syntax.. For roots other than 2 use: sqrt[3](x). For logarithms other than the natural logarithm use: log[2](x). For fractions use 1 // 2. For variables with subscripts use: rho:fluid.
